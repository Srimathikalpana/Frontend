Past saturday we went to Wonderla which is located in Cochin,Kerala. So I'm here to share my experience

WONDERLA

TRAVEL

We were taken to Wonderla by Star health team lead.Many of my father's collegues joined us with their family. This trip was well arranged by Express holidays.We started to Cochin on friday night around 11.00pm. Then we reached Cochin by 7.30 on the morning!
We went in an A/c bus!

ARRANGEMENTS

After we reached the place, they alloted us a room for refreshment...They also arranged breakfast for us! then we bought some nylon dresses which are preffered to wear there! After buying dresses ,while going back to room we got stuck with rain.
Finally we entered into the wonderful Wonderla :)

DRY GAMES

We played many dry games in which some of then were daring too!

  • Flash tower
  • Maverick
  • Equinox
  • Space gun
  • Recoil
    • etc...
But the fun is, we played all dry games by getting wet in rain!

WET GAMES

As well we played in all water games.Actually we spent more time in climbing stairs for slides and thrilling water games than the time we spent in water (LOL :D)

Here are some of the we enjoyed:
  • Wave pool I & II
  • Rain disco
  • Twister
  • Korneto
  • Fun glides
  • Boomerang
  • Wonder splash
    • etc...

BACK HOME

Then we get back to Salem...On our way, we ate our dinner at Rahmath hotel. We reached Salem around 5.00 am on Sunday!

Had a great experience :D
