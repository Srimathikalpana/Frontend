Salem, natively spelt as Selam[note 1] (pronounced [selam] (listen)), is a major city in Salem district, located on the banks of Thirumanimutharu river in the Indian state of Tamil Nadu. Salem is the Fifth largest urban agglomeration and metropolitan city in the state by next to Chennai, Coimbatore, Madurai, Tiruchirappalli in Tamil Nadu. Its area covering is 124 km2 (48 sq mi).[8] Salem city has Major business in Silver Anklets, Sago, Mangoes, Steel, Textiles and larger exported value.
